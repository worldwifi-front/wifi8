شبكة Wi-Fi مجانية لامركزية معتمدة على تكنولوجيا سلسلة الكتل

World Wi-Fi is a global high-tech project being built by the efforts of the community. We create a worldwide decentralized free Wi-Fi network on private routers (most of which are private routers). Router owners can distribute free Internet and earn a cryptocurrency on advertising on the internet. In the end, everyone wins: wi-fi users don’t have to pay for the Internet, router owners receive revenue, and advertisers broadcast advertising to their target audience by paying to the router owners.

At the moment, World Wi-Fi platform is on pre-SALE, and you can get a 15%-25% bonus.
