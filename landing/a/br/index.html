Usando a plataforma World WI-FI, os usuários podem se conectar à rede WI-FI aberta gratuitamente. Será suficiente apenas ver um pequeno vídeo promocional antes de conectar.
Cada proprietário de roteador pode instalar nosso software em poucos cliques, criar uma rede aberta isolada de sua rede privada e ganhar uma moeda criptografada. Outros usuários se conectarão a sua rede aberta, quando conectados, será transmitida a publicidade e os anunciantes pagarão ao proprietário do roteador por cada visualização da publicidade.
Os anunciantes com a ajuda da World WI-FI poderão escolher seu público e transmitir publicidade para ele. Em nossa plataforma, os anunciantes receberão:.
- dados de segmentação precisos,
- estatísticas confiáveis ligadas ao blockchain, na conversão da campanha publicitária gastada em cada ponto de WI-FI,
disponibilidade de tráfego vivo e ausência completa de bots.

Além disso, é resolvido o problema que é relevante para a maioria dos provedores de Internet – a cobrança de atrasos.

Participants of the platform are casual users, router owners and advertisers. An invite is always required to get connected to the system. An invite is a referral linked to the user's wallet in the system. The referral program works like this: all wallets remit 25% of the cost of the ad shown on the router to those who have joined them. So, the router owners are interested in scaling the system up. Everyone can use his/her invite to connect new participants and get revenue from the ads shared by their routers. It is all about allocating the advertising budget only – the router owners do not pay anything to each other. First invites will be given to PRE-SALE /TOKEN SALE participants and the project team to develop the system (root invites). The only difference in root invites is that their holders do not pay anyone interest on the ads shared via their router. A root invite is similar to a digital franchise with 0% fee, with which you can develop the project and raise maximum revenue. The number of root invites is limited and, if you are not planning to pursue the project, you will be able to sell it with profit after the TOKEN SALE to an active community member who wants to earn on the network development. To join the system, you can use a root invite received at the TOKEN SALE or a basic invite provided by another user. Once the platform is launched, anyone will be able to get a basic invite on the World Wi-Fi website.
Two working ad selling businesses and hotspots. In B2B segment, the model is proven and operating for 2 years already. There are in total about 14,000 points connected in 80 cities.
Blockchain is used for storing trusted information on payments and accounts. All transactions are made in WeToken currency. Every member of the network – hotspot, advertiser, network developer and investor – has a WeTokens account. The blockchain stores the info: • how may WeTokens a member has, • on active Deals: information on targets, statistics, a link to expanded Deal data in the ad statistics database, • on Hotspots: links to their statistics and accounts. The blockchain is the only place in the whole web where operations with WeTokens are made. A WeToken smart contract is a guarantee that payments will be remitted when the Deal's target is reached.
